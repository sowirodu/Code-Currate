About Us
Welcome to CodeCurate, Seth Owirodu, a Trinity University Computer Science undergraduate. Inspired by the gaps he identified in academic resources, Seth embarked on a mission to revolutionize the way computer science students engage with Data Structures and Algorithms. CodeCurate offers an all-in-one platform for students to explore, question, and challenge themselves, ensuring a profound understanding of key computational concepts. Our vision is not just to be a tool, but a movement towards accessible, quality education in computer science. Join us in redefining the future of learning.
